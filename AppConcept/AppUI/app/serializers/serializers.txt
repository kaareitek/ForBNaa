Serializers manipulate the incoming data from the back-end API. For this project, care has been
taken to make sure that the back-end adheres to one of Ember's built in Serializers.
Serializers are completely customizable, but it is recommended to simply feed Ember the proper
data formatting from the back-end. More can be read at https://guides.emberjs.com/release/models/customizing-serializers/
